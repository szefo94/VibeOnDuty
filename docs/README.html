Browser FPS · Three.js

VIBEON DUTY

A browser-based first-person shooter built with Three.js. Survive endless waves, challenge yourself in Adaptive mode, coordinate in Team Deathmatch, go head-to-head in Search & Destroy, or sharpen your aim in the Training Range.

01

Game Modes

Survival

Wave Mode — Incursion

Survive endless waves of enemies in a tactical indoor arena. Clear each wave to face the next. A persistent drone hunts you between waves.

5v5

Team Deathmatch

5v5 bot battle. You and four green-tinted allies face five red-tinted enemies. First team to 50 kills wins. All bots pathfind, fight, and respawn.

Tactical · Best-of-7

Search & Destroy

Plant the bomb at Site A or B, or stop the enemy team from planting. First to 4 round wins takes the match. Full buy phase economy.

Auto-scaling

Adaptive Mode

Incursion with silent difficulty scaling. AI tracks rolling K/D over 60 s, re-evaluates every 30 s. Level 1 = Recruit; Level 10 = Elite. Starts at 5.

Skills

Training Range

56×80 m shooting hall. 9 targets across short / medium / long zones. Four drills, four difficulty tiers, three movement presets. Infinite ammo.

S&D Details

10 s buy phase · 60 s round timer · 40 s fuse after plant · 3 s plant / 5 s defuse (hold G)

Round ends on: bomb exploded · bomb defused · team elimination · round timeout

03

Features

World

Five maps: Greek Columns, Rooftop District, Column Arena, Vanguard Complex (3-floor), Training Range
24×24 tile arenas — solid walls, cracked walls (crawl gaps), ramps, pillars, multi-level heightmap
Bomb sites A/B with pulsing ring markers and point lights in S&D mode
Torch lights with flicker, PCF soft shadows, exponential fog, ACES filmic tone mapping

Player

Full movement: walk, sprint, crouch, slide, slide-cancel jump, dive, lean
Gravity, jump, step-height traversal on ramps and ledges
EMP slow (drone ability): reduces movement speed to 40% for 2 s
1st-person death animation: weapon tips forward and drops on death

Difficulty

TierReact TimeDamageSpeed
Recruit900–1800 ms6×0.60
Regular400–950 ms10×1.00
Veteran150–380 ms16×1.25

Enemy AI

Patrol waypoints with idle wander rotation
Line-of-sight detection with difficulty-scaled reaction delay
A* pathfinding, throttled to once per 600–800 ms or on goal change
Velocity-based movement with acceleration + drag; knockback stagger on hit
Friendly bots — A*-pathfind to sites/enemies, shoot with LOS check, scout when idle
Sniper bots — hold position, rotate to face player only, never advance

Drone AI

Persistent drone (wave): orbits at ~8 unit radius, EMP pulse at <30% HP; tumbles with smoke trail on death
S&D / TDM recon drones — one per team; waypoint patrol, periodic LOS scan
Friendly drone reveals enemy bot positions on minimap
Enemy drone compromises player position and alerts nearby bots

Camera

1st person: head bob, weapon sway, spray cone, recoil
3rd person over-the-shoulder (Fortnite-style): animated transition, shoulder swap, ADS preserved
Lean: camera rolls ±0.28 rad and shifts ±0.38 units sideways
AWP scope: dark radial vignette, Brownian sway oscillator damped by hold-breath (Shift), +9.8 m/s² bullet drop

HUD

Ammo ring, energy ring, HP segments, reload bar, spray cone indicator
S&D match header: role · round · score · timer; plant / defuse bars; bomb fuse countdown
TDM score bar: team kills vs enemy kills · timer · kill limit
Minimap radar: sweep animation, fog of war, enemy/drone/ammo blips
Floating world-space damage numbers (yellow enemy / red player)
Training Range HUD: drill name, timer/score, hits, accuracy, headshots, streak, avg reaction

Running Locally

$npm install
$npm run dev # dev server at http://localhost:5173
$npm run build # production build → dist/
$npm test # Vitest unit tests
$npm run test:e2e # Playwright smoke tests
$npm run lint # ESLint
$npm run typecheck # tsc --noEmit
